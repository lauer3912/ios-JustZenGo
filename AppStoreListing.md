## Full Description

**100+ features — the most complete focus app ever made.**

JustZen takes the classic Pomodoro technique to a whole new level. AI-adaptive smarts, immersive ambient sounds, detailed progress tracking, and a design you'll actually love — all to help you rebuild your focus.

- AI-Adaptive Pomodoro — smart work/rest adjustment

- 100+ Achievement Badges — from "First Focus" to "100-Hour Master"
- Focus Streak Wall — GitHub-style heatmap
- Deep Data Insights — daily/weekly/monthly reports
- Rolling Pomodoro — continuous focus mode
- Daily Challenges — micro focus challenges every day
- Focus Intelligence Dashboard
- Wind Down Mode
