## Description

**100+ features — the most complete focus app ever made.**

JustZen takes the classic Pomodoro technique to a whole new level. AI-adaptive smarts, immersive ambient sounds, detailed progress tracking, and a design you'll actually love — all to help you rebuild your focus.

---

### 🎯 Core Features

**• AI-Adaptive Pomodoro**
Smart work/rest duration adjustment that optimizes in real-time based on your focus state and historical performance.

**• 12 Immersive Ambient Sounds**
Rain, forest, ocean waves, coffee shop, white noise... All sounds are synthesized in real-time with AVAudioEngine — no internet required.

**• 100+ Achievement Badges**
From "First Focus" to "100-Hour Master" — collect badges that make your progress visible and rewarding.

**• Focus Streak Wall**
GitHub-style heatmap showing your daily focus journey. Streak check-ins motivate you to maintain habits.

**• Deep Data Insights**
Daily/weekly/monthly focus reports. Discover your best focus hours and patterns.

**• Rolling Pomodoro**
Continuous focus mode with seamless work sessions and brief pauses only when needed.

**• Daily Challenges**
A unique micro focus challenge every day. Complete it for bonus rewards.
