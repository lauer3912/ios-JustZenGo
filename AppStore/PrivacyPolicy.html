JustZen

Privacy Policy · Last Updated: April 12, 2026

In short: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, track, or sell any personal information.

1. What We Collect

JustZen processes as much data locally as possible. The information we collect is minimal:

  • Local usage data — Focus session records, achievement progress, and settings preferences (stored on your device only)
  • Crash logs — Anonymous technical information when the app crashes (used to improve app quality)
  • Analytics (opt-in) — If you choose to opt in, we collect anonymous usage statistics to understand which features are most popular

2. What We Don't Collect

We explicitly commit to never collecting or accessing:

  • Your personal identity information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Your location data
  • Your contacts, calendar, or messages
  • Your activity in other apps
  • Your browsing history or web access records
  • Any data that can identify you

3. Data Storage

Your focus data is stored locally on your device by default, using UserDefaults or local database.

If you subscribe to Premium and enable iCloud sync, your data syncs through your personal iCloud account. Data associated with your Apple ID is protected by Apple's privacy policy.

4. Third-Party Services

JustZen uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple Store Connect — For in-app purchases and subscription management (governed by Apple's privacy policy)
  • CloudKit — Optional iCloud data sync (governed by Apple's privacy policy)

We don't share data with any advertising networks, data brokers, or third-party analytics companies.

5. Children's Privacy

JustZen is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including device-side encryption (HTTPS in transit) and secure server infrastructure (where applicable).

7. Your Rights

Depending on applicable laws in your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent (including canceling subscriptions)
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact: privacy@focusTimer.app

8. Policy Changes

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the update in the app and update the "Last Updated" date at the top. Significant changes will be communicated in advance.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, contact us:

Email: privacy@focusTimer.app

JustZen is crafted with care by an independent developer. We believe privacy is a fundamental right, not an optional feature.
